For you

A Birthday Letter

On the occasion of your birthday

Dear loved one,

Happy birthday. Not simply to another year, but to you. To the life you have carried, the storms you have crossed, the joy you have given, and the quiet becoming that is still unfolding inside you. This letter is not advice you must obey. It is a small lantern. Hold it lightly. Let it remind you of what is already true.

I

You Belong to the Whole Sky

You do not need to make yourself smaller to belong. You do not need one perfect label, one perfect path, or one perfect answer. At the centre of every sincere path is the same quiet invitation: come closer to love, come closer to truth, come closer to the part of you that has never been broken. You are allowed to be curious. You are allowed to gather wisdom from many places. You are allowed to listen for what feels honest in your own chest. The world is wide, and so are you. You are not spiritually lost. You are learning the shape of your own home.

II

Gratitude Begins Where You Stand

Gratitude is not pretending that everything is easy. It is not forcing a smile over pain. It is the gentle practice of stopping long enough to notice what still remains. Breath. Light. A hand that once helped you. A song that once found you at the right time. A small kindness. A second chance. A morning that arrived without asking you to earn it. You do not have to wait for your life to become perfect before you can receive it. You can begin here, exactly here, with what is still good, still possible, and still quietly offering itself to you.

III

Your Life Is Still Becoming

Every life has chapters that feel too heavy while they are being lived. But a chapter is not the whole story. You have already changed in ways your younger self could not have imagined. You have survived days that once looked impossible. You have become softer in places, stronger in others, and wiser in ways that do not always announce themselves. On your birthday, remember this: you are not late. You are not behind. You are still being shaped by love, choice, courage, rest, laughter, and time. The next part of your life is allowed to surprise you beautifully.

With all my love,

always in your corner,

and with deep faith in who you are becoming,

Callan
